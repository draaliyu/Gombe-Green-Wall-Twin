NDVI class distribution

How to interpret this view

Display classNDVI intervalWhat it can suggestWhat it cannot prove
Bare / very sparse< 0.15Low spectral greenness in the selected mosaic.Active desertification, soil fertility or land-use cause.
Sparse0.15–0.30Limited or discontinuous green vegetation.Specific crop/tree condition without field data.
Moderate0.30–0.50Moderate photosynthetically active cover.Forest density, biomass or restoration success.
Dense≥ 0.50Relatively strong greenness in valid pixels.Tree species, planted status or permanent cover.
The class boundaries are visualisation thresholds for this demonstrator. Operational monitoring requires local calibration, repeat observations and field verification.
